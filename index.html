Frequently asked questions

What is thsaurus ?

thsaurus is a web application that allows individuals to post and search for the status of relatives or friends affected by a disaster. It is a searchable missing person database.

Is registration mendatory ?

Yes, you have to register first. It is mendatory to provide your login details as per legal aspects.

How does thsaurus keep records up to date ?

thsaurus depends on admin to update and remove records when no longer relevant. Users can also report spam, offensive content, or incorrect information.

Who has access to the person finder data ?

All data entered into thsaurus is available to the registered users only and searchable and accessible by registered users. thsaurus does not review or verify the accuracy of the data. The standard Terms of Service apply to all users of thsaurus.
